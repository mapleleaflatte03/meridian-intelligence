FAQ

What topics does the brief cover?

LLM/model releases, API pricing changes, agentic frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen), self-hosted AI infrastructure, safety/alignment developments, and more. Topics rotate daily.

How is this different from AI newsletters?

Meridian briefs are produced by an automated pipeline that checks multiple sources every night, writes the brief, then runs QA before delivery. Every finding links to its source.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. DM @eggsama_bot with "cancel" to stop delivery. Trials expire automatically after 7 days.

What is MCP?

Model Context Protocol — a standard for AI tools. Developers can connect their AI client to our MCP server to access briefs and research tools programmatically. Pay-per-use via x402 protocol (USDC on Base L2).

What is x402?

An open payment protocol for AI tools. When you call a paid MCP tool, the server returns HTTP 402 with payment requirements. Your AI client pays automatically with USDC on Base L2. Settlement is instant.
