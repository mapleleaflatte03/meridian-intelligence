FAQ

What competitors can I track?

Any company in the AI/ML ecosystem: model providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, Meta, Cohere, DeepSeek), frameworks (LangChain, LlamaIndex, CrewAI), infrastructure (Cloudflare, AWS, Azure, GCP), and more. Custom competitor tracking available on request.

What topics do you monitor?

Pricing changes, model/product launches, API updates, deprecations, partnerships, positioning shifts, and strategic moves. You can configure which topics matter for each tracked competitor.

How is this different from a newsletter or Google Alerts?

Meridian monitors structured sources (pricing pages, changelogs, API docs) that newsletters and Google Alerts miss. Every finding cites its source with a date. Output is structured for PMM workflows — not just raw links.

What is a battlecard?

A structured competitive reference: recent moves, pricing intelligence, product updates, strategic signals, and talking points. The standard format used by PMM and sales enablement teams to brief reps on competitors.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. DM @eggsama_bot with "cancel" to stop delivery. Trials expire automatically after 7 days.

What is MCP?

Model Context Protocol — a standard for AI tools. Developers can connect their AI client to our MCP server to access intelligence tools programmatically. Pay-per-use via x402 protocol (USDC on Base L2).
